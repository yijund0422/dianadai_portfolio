Bridging the Gap Between Technology and Learning

With 8+ years of experience in learning design and a unique background in both education and technology, I specialize in creating learning experiences that are both pedagogically sound and technologically innovative.

My journey started as a software developer, where I discovered my passion for making complex concepts accessible. This led me to pursue instructional design, where I could combine my technical skills with learning science to create truly impactful educational experiences.

I believe great learning design happens at the intersection of empathy, creativity, and data. Every project I work on is grounded in user research, informed by learning science, and optimized through continuous iteration.
